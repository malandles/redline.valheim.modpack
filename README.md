# Redline's Valheim Modpack

## Required

BepInExPack Valheim - https://valheim.thunderstore.io/package/denikson/BepInExPack_Valheim/
> BepInEx pack for Valheim. Preconfigured and includes unstripped Unity DLLs.

BetterNetworking Valheim - https://valheim.thunderstore.io/package/CW_Jesse/BetterNetworking_Valheim/
> If you've been experiencing lag or desync when playing with your friends, this is the solution!

EpicLoot - https://valheim.thunderstore.io/package/RandyKnapp/EpicLoot/
> This mod aims to add a loot drop experience to Valheim similar to Diablo or other RPGs. Monsters and chests can now drop Magic, Rare, Epic, or Legendary magic items. Each magic item has a number of magic effects on it, that give bonuses to the item or your character when that magic item is equipped.

ReviveAllies - https://valheim.thunderstore.io/package/Ratzu/ReviveAllies
> Allows you to revive your friends at their tombstone within a revive window.


## UI

BetterUI Reforged - https://valheim.thunderstore.io/package/thedefside/BetterUI_Reforged/
> Masa's BetterUI mod updated with bug fixes and new features


## Quality of life

AAA_Crafting - https://valheim.thunderstore.io/package/Azumatt/AAA_Crafting/
> Azu Anti-Arthritic Crafting. Gives you an input field to enter the amount of items you want to craft. Instead of having to click the craft button 100 times or a fancy plus button 5-10 times.

AzuWorkbench Inventory Repair - https://valheim.thunderstore.io/package/Azumatt/AzuWorkbench_Inventory_Repair/
> Automatically repair your items when you interact with a workbench or crafting table.

BetterArchery - https://valheim.thunderstore.io/package/ishid4/BetterArchery/
> Adds many features and changes to bow gameplay.

Build_Camera - https://valheim.thunderstore.io/package/gittywithexcitement/Build_Camera/
> Adds a build camera to make building easier, to simplify getting the right perspective so that you can see the snap point. No more scaffolding.

BuildHelper - https://valheim.thunderstore.io/package/Xenofell/BuildHelper/
> BuildHelper provides some handy tweaks to help make builder's lives easier.

DodgeOnDoubleTap - https://valheim.thunderstore.io/package/RoundStone/DodgeOnDoubleTap/
> 4 directional roll by double tapping WASD keys.

FloatingItems - https://valheim.thunderstore.io/package/castix/FloatingItems
> This simple mod makes all items float on the water surface.

MoreSlots - https://valheim.thunderstore.io/package/Fang86/MoreSlots
> This mod adds extra rows into your inventory. You can change this to any number you want - equal to or over 4. By default, the mod adds 2 rows (16 slots).

Quick Stack Store Sort Trash Restock - https://valheim.thunderstore.io/package/Goldenrevolver/Quick_Stack_Store_Sort_Trash_Restock
> Quick Stacking, Sorting, Trashing and more in one cohesive package

RequipMe - https://valheim.thunderstore.io/package/Neobotics/RequipMe
> Instantly re-equip from your tombstone!

SimpleRecycling_Fixed - https://valheim.thunderstore.io/package/remmiz/SimpleRecycling_Fixed
> Adds a tab to the crafting station that recycles (uncrafts) items from player inventory

SNEAKer - https://valheim.thunderstore.io/package/blacks7ar/SNEAKer
> A simple mod that makes your sneaking speed scaled by your Sneak skill level.

SpeedyPaths - https://valheim.thunderstore.io/package/Nextek/SpeedyPaths/
> Gives more purpose to paths. Increases player speed on paths and constructions. Reduces stamina consumed while sprinting on paths. Exposes optional modifiers for regular terrain in each biome. All values are tuneable.

TorchesEternal - https://valheim.thunderstore.io/package/Xenofell/TorchesEternal
> Torches and other fire sources that require fuel (campfire, hearth, etc) will burn infinitely when using Torches Eternal.

UseEquipmentInWater - https://valheim.thunderstore.io/package/LVH-IT/UseEquipmentInWater
> Allows you to use your Equipment while swimming in water


## Farming

MassFarming - https://valheim.thunderstore.io/package/k942/MassFarming

PlantEverything - https://valheim.thunderstore.io/package/Advize/PlantEverything

## Custom Skills

Blacksmithing - https://valheim.thunderstore.io/package/Smoothbrain/Blacksmithing
> Adds a skill that increases the durability of armor and weapons and other perks for blacksmithing.

Building - https://valheim.thunderstore.io/package/Smoothbrain/Building
> Adds a skill that increases the health of pieces built by you.

CartographySkill - https://valheim.thunderstore.io/package/Advize/CartographySkill/
> Adds the cartography skill, allowing you to increase your map explore radius as you uncover more of your map. Also allows you to craft a functional spyglass to see things from afar.

Cooking - https://valheim.thunderstore.io/package/Smoothbrain/Cooking/
> Adds a skill that increases health, stamina and HP regeneration for cooked food.

Evasion - https://valheim.thunderstore.io/package/Smoothbrain/Evasion/
> Adds a skill that reduces the stamina usage of dodging.

Farming - https://valheim.thunderstore.io/package/Smoothbrain/Farming
> Adds a skill that makes crops grow faster and increases item yield for harvesting crops.

Foraging - https://valheim.thunderstore.io/package/Smoothbrain/Foraging/
> Adds a skill that increases item yield while foraging and makes berries and mushrooms respawn faster.

Lumberjacking - https://valheim.thunderstore.io/package/Smoothbrain/Lumberjacking
> Replaces the wood cutting skill with a Lumberjacking skill.

Mining - https://valheim.thunderstore.io/package/Smoothbrain/Mining
> Adds a skill that increases damage dealt while mining and item yield from ore deposits.

PackHorse - https://valheim.thunderstore.io/package/Smoothbrain/PackHorse
> Adds a skill that increases the maximum carry weight.

Ranching - https://valheim.thunderstore.io/package/Smoothbrain/Ranching
> Adds a skill that makes taming animals faster and increases item yield of tamed creatures.

Sailing - https://valheim.thunderstore.io/package/Smoothbrain/Sailing
> Adds a skill that increases the health of ships, sailing speed and exploration radius while sailing.

Tenacity - https://valheim.thunderstore.io/package/Smoothbrain/Tenacity
> Adds a skill that reduces damage taken. Skill increases by taking damage.

VitalitySkill - https://valheim.thunderstore.io/package/Hub/VitalitySkill/
> Increase MaxHealth, MaxStamina, FoodTimeTicker, StaminaRegen, Runspeed, Walkspeed, CarryWeight in scale with the VitalitySkill, Have an Selfheal and reduce many stamina drains.
